# Temporal reasoning
After the storm passed the streets were covered in fallen branches
Before she left she made sure all the windows were closed
While he was cooking dinner she set the table and poured the water
As soon as the alarm went off he jumped out of bed and got dressed
By the time they arrived the party was already in full swing
During the meeting she took careful notes on everything that was said
Once the sun set the temperature dropped quickly and they lit a fire
Until the results came back nobody knew whether it had worked
Whenever it rains heavily the basement floods and they have to pump it out
Since she moved to the city she has been much happier with her life
