# Narrative text
The cat chased the mouse and the mouse ran under the table
Once upon a time a young knight set out to find the golden sword
She walked through the forest until she came to a clearing with a cottage
The old man sat by the fire and told stories of his youth
The ship sailed into the harbor just as the storm began to clear
He opened the letter and read the words that would change his life
The children ran out of school and into the bright afternoon sun
She looked out the window and saw the first snow of winter falling
